HIPPOWASTE’sTM new plasterboard recycling and collection service will collect your waste plasterboard from site for as little as £25 a bag*. The plasterboard recycling service from Hippowaste offers:

  • a clear price per bag with no tonnage surcharges
  • collection of any uncontaminated plasterboard
  • a comprehensive nationwide collection service from a single supplier
  • 100% recycling of plasterboard, reducing your waste to landfill
  • and you’re not tied to any brand or manufacturer take-back scheme

Plasterboard Recycling Zones

The plasterboard recycling service is available nationwide, but due to the differing costs of plasterboard disposal in different areas, pricing is affected by the site location. To keep this illustration simple we have displayed pricing based on postcode regions, but please contact us to discuss your specific site if it is in Zone 3 as some postcode regions are large and pricing varies across them.

Zone 1 | Price per bag: £25
Postcode regions: AL, B, BH, BL, BN, BR, BS, CR, DA, DE, DN, DY, E, EC, EN, GU, HA, HP, IG, KT, L, LU, M, MK, N, NG, NW, OL, PO, RH, RM, S, SE, SL, SM, SO, SW, TN, TW, UB, W, WA, WC, WD, WN, WS, WV.

Zone 2 | Price per bag: £30
Postcode regions: BA, BB, BD, CB, CF, CM, CT, CV, CW, DH, DT, FY, GL, HD, HU, HX, LE, LN, LS, ME, NE, NN, NP, OX, PR, RG, SG, SK, SN, SP, SR, SS, ST, TA, TF, TS, WF, WR, YO.

Zone 3 | Price per bag: POA*
Postcode regions: AB, CA, CH, CO, DD, DG, DL, EH,EX, FK, G, HG, HR, HS, IP, IV, KA, KW, KY, LA, LD, LL, ML, NR, PA, PE, PH, PL, SA, SY, TD, TQ, TR.
